Bezier Curves

How do I add new points to the bezier curve?

Just double click onto the curve. The new point will be added nearby the clicked position.

How do I remove bezier points?

Click onto the bezier curve point you want to remove. It should be marked as selected. Then just press the [DELETE] key and the point will be removed.
Note that at least two points are required to define a bezier curve. The last two points cannot be removed!
